About us

"Reducing food waste is not just about saving food and money; it's also about preserving the planet and its resources. When we waste food, we're not just throwing away the food itself, but also the water, energy, and other resources that went into producing, transporting, and storing it. By reducing food waste, we can help conserve our planet's natural resources and build a more sustainable future for everyone."

- Dana Gunders, Executive Director of ReFED

The fight on food waste

We're passionate about providing our clients with fresh, high-quality fruit and vegetables delivered straight to their doorstep. But our mission goes beyond just making healthy food more convenient - we're also committed to reducing food waste and doing our part to help the planet.

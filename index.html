Meet our Fruit & Veggie boxes

Dinky apples. Curvy courgettes. And a bunch of beautiful beetroot, stalks and all. Our fruit and veg delivery boxes are packed with all kinds of delicious produce rescued fresh from the farm, including the “too odd” and “too many” at risk of going to waste. There are three sizes of fruit and veg box to pick from, and you can choose from weekly or fortnightly deliveries.
